Most issues with Trxphy can be resolved quickly with a few targeted steps. This guide walks you through the most common problems users encounter — from login and integration hiccups to notification gaps and billing questions — so you can get back on track without waiting for support.

## Login and Access Issues

### Forgot Your Password

If you can't remember your password, resetting it takes less than a minute.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the login page">
    Navigate to the Trxphy login page and click **Forgot password?** below the sign-in form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email address">
    Type the email address associated with your Trxphy account and click **Send reset link**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your inbox">
    Open your email inbox and look for a message from Trxphy with the subject line "Reset your password." The link is valid for 30 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    Click the link in the email, enter a new password, confirm it, and click **Save**. You'll be signed in automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Account Locked

After 5 consecutive failed login attempts, Trxphy temporarily locks your account to protect against unauthorized access. The lock lifts automatically after **15 minutes**. If you don't want to wait, use the **Forgot password?** flow above to reset your credentials and regain access immediately.

### Not Receiving Verification Emails

If you're not receiving account verification or confirmation emails from Trxphy:

- **Check your spam or junk folder** — emails from `noreply@trxphy.com` are occasionally filtered.
- **Add the sender to your contacts** to prevent future filtering.
- **Request a new verification email** from the login page by clicking **Resend verification email**.
- If your email provider uses strict filtering, ask your IT team to allowlist the `trxphy.com` domain.

## Integration Issues

### Slack Not Posting Notifications

If your Slack integration is connected but messages aren't appearing in your channel:

- Verify that the Trxphy app has **permission to post** in the selected channel. For private channels, the Trxphy app must be explicitly invited.
- Check that the correct channel is selected under **Settings → Integrations → Slack**.
- Disconnect and reconnect the integration: go to **Settings → Integrations**, click **Disconnect** next to Slack, then click **Connect** and follow the authorization steps again.
- Confirm that your Slack workspace admin hasn't restricted third-party app posting.

### Google Calendar Not Syncing

If your goals and milestones aren't appearing in Google Calendar:

- Go to **Settings → Integrations → Google Calendar** and click **Re-authorize** to refresh your access token, which can expire over time.
- After re-authorizing, confirm that the correct calendar is selected in the integration settings — Trxphy syncs to a specific calendar, not all calendars in your account.
- Make sure the milestones you expect to see have due dates set, as only dated items sync to Google Calendar.

## Notification Issues

### Not Receiving Email Notifications

If you're not getting Trxphy notification emails:

- **Check your spam folder** for emails from `notifications@trxphy.com`.
- Confirm your email address is verified by visiting [Settings → Profile](/settings/profile).
- Review your notification preferences — some notification types may be turned off. See the [Notifications guide](/guides/notifications) for a full walkthrough of available settings.
- If you recently changed your email address, verify the new address from the confirmation email that was sent to it.

### Push Notifications Not Showing

If browser push notifications from Trxphy aren't appearing on your device:

- Check that your **browser has permission** to send notifications from Trxphy. In Chrome, go to **Settings → Privacy and security → Site settings → Notifications** and make sure `trxphy.com` is set to **Allow**.
- In Firefox, check **Preferences → Privacy & Security → Permissions → Notifications**.
- On macOS, confirm that your browser is allowed to send notifications in **System Settings → Notifications**.
- If you previously blocked notifications, you'll need to manually re-enable permission — Trxphy cannot prompt you again once permission has been denied.

## Billing Issues

### Payment Failed

If a payment attempt fails, your account will remain active briefly while you update your details. To resolve it:

- Go to [Settings → Billing](/settings/billing) and click **Update payment method**.
- Enter a new valid card or update your existing card's expiry date and CVV.
- Once updated, click **Retry payment** to immediately retry the failed charge.
- If the issue persists, contact your bank to confirm the card isn't being blocked for online transactions.

### Charged After Cancellation

If you see a charge after cancelling your subscription, it's most likely a prorated charge or a charge that was already in progress before cancellation was processed. Downgrades and cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period, so a final charge for the active period is expected. If you believe the charge is an error, please [contact support](/help/contact) with your invoice number so the team can investigate.
