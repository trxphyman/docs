The Trxphy support team is here to help when you run into something you can't resolve on your own. Before you reach out, taking a moment to check the self-serve resources below can often get you an answer faster — but when you do need to talk to a human, here's everything you need to know about getting in touch.

## Before You Reach Out

Many common questions and issues are already covered in the documentation. Checking these first can save you time:

- **Search the FAQ** — Visit the [FAQ](/help/faq) page for answers to the most common questions about plans, billing, privacy, and features.
- **Try the Troubleshooting guide** — The [Troubleshooting](/help/troubleshooting) guide covers step-by-step fixes for login issues, integration errors, notification problems, and billing concerns.

If you've checked both resources and still need help, the team is ready to assist.

## Response Times

Response times vary by plan. The table below shows what to expect after submitting a support request.

| Plan | Response Time | Support Type |
|------|--------------|--------------|
| Free | 3–5 business days | Email |
| Pro | 1 business day | Email + Chat |
| Team | 4 hours | Priority Email + Chat |

Response times reflect business hours (Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM UTC). Requests submitted outside of business hours will be picked up on the next business day, though Team plan priority requests are reviewed more frequently.

## What to Include in Your Request

Providing the right information upfront helps the support team diagnose and resolve your issue without back-and-forth delays. When you contact support, please include:

- **Account email address** — the email you use to log in to Trxphy
- **Current plan** — Free, Pro, or Team
- **Browser and device** — e.g., Chrome 124 on macOS 14, or Safari on iPhone iOS 17
- **Steps to reproduce** — a clear, numbered list of what you did before encountering the issue
- **Screenshots or screen recordings** — if applicable, these speed up diagnosis significantly
- **Error messages** — copy the exact text of any error messages you see

<Tip>
  Pro and Team plan users can skip the email queue for many issues. Access **live chat** directly from the in-app **Help widget** (the question mark icon in the bottom-right corner of the Trxphy dashboard) to connect with the support team in real time during business hours.
</Tip>

<Note>
  For billing disputes or questions about a specific charge, always reference your **invoice number** in your support request. You can find invoice numbers in **Settings → Billing → Invoices**. Including it allows the team to locate your transaction immediately and resolve the issue faster.
</Note>
