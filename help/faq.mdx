Trxphy is built to be straightforward, but you may still have questions as you get started or grow your usage. Below you'll find answers to the most common questions our users ask, organized by topic so you can jump straight to what matters most.

## Getting Started

<Accordion title="Is there a free plan?">
  Yes! Trxphy's Free plan lets you create and track up to 3 active goals at no cost. You get access to core goal management and progress tracking features, making it a great way to explore the platform before committing to a paid plan. If you need more, upgrading to Pro or Team unlocks unlimited goals and additional features.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do I need a credit card to sign up?">
  No credit card is required to sign up for the Free plan. Just create your account with an email address and password — no payment information needed. You'll only be prompted for billing details if you choose to upgrade to a Pro or Team plan.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I import goals from another app?">
  Yes, Trxphy supports CSV import so you can bring in goals and milestones from other tools. Navigate to **Settings → Import**, upload your formatted CSV file, and map the columns to Trxphy's fields. This is a great way to migrate from a spreadsheet or another goal-tracking app without starting from scratch.
</Accordion>

## Goals and Tracking

<Accordion title="How many goals can I have?">
  The number of active goals depends on your plan:

  - **Free:** Up to 3 active goals
  - **Pro:** Unlimited active goals
  - **Team:** Unlimited active goals, shared across your workspace

  Completed goals are archived and don't count toward your active goal limit, so you can keep your workspace focused without losing your history.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I share a goal with someone?">
  Yes, you can share goals in two ways. If you're on a Team plan, you can collaborate on goals directly within a shared team workspace where members can view and update progress together. Alternatively, you can generate a shareable link for any goal, allowing others to view it without needing a Trxphy account. You control visibility and access at all times.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens to completed goals?">
  When you mark a goal as complete, it moves to the **Archive** view rather than being deleted. You can access all of your completed goals from Archive at any time to review your history, celebrate past wins, or reference milestones you've already achieved. Archived goals do not count against your active goal limit.
</Accordion>

## Billing

<Accordion title="Can I switch plans at any time?">
  Yes, you can change your plan at any time from **Settings → Billing**. Upgrades take effect immediately — you'll gain access to new features right away, and your billing will be prorated for the remainder of the current period. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period, so you keep full access to your current plan's features until then.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you offer refunds?">
  Refund requests are handled on a case-by-case basis. If you believe you're entitled to a refund — for example, due to an accidental charge or a billing error — please reach out to the support team directly. Visit the [Contact](/help/contact) page to submit your request with your account details and invoice number, and the team will get back to you as quickly as possible.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
  Trxphy accepts all major credit and debit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. Payments are processed securely through **Stripe**, a leading payment infrastructure provider. Trxphy does not store your full card details — all sensitive payment information is handled directly by Stripe.
</Accordion>

## Privacy

<Accordion title="Is my data private?">
  Yes, your goals and data are private by default. No one else can see your goals unless you explicitly invite team members to your workspace or share a goal via a shareable link. Trxphy does not sell your data or make your goals visible to other users without your direct action. You remain in full control of who sees your information at all times.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I export my data?">
  Yes, you can export all of your Trxphy data at any time. Go to **Settings → Export** and choose your preferred format — either **CSV** for spreadsheet compatibility or **JSON** for developer use. Your export will include your goals, milestones, and progress history. This ensures you always have a portable copy of your data, regardless of your plan.
</Accordion>
