## Get Started in Three Steps

New to Trxphy? Here's how quickly you can go from sign-up to actively tracking your goals.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Create your free Trxphy account at [trxphy.com](https://trxphy.com). All you need is an email address — no credit card required to get started.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Your Account">
    Complete your profile, configure your preferences, and connect any tools you already use. Visit the [Account Setup](/account-setup) guide for a full walkthrough of every option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Tracking">
    Create your first goal, break it into milestones, and watch your progress come to life on your personal dashboard. Check out [Your First Steps](/guides/first-steps) for tips on making the most of your setup.
  </Step>
</Steps>
