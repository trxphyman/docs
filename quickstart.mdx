Trxphy gets you from zero to tracking in minutes. By the end of this guide, you will have a verified account, a complete profile, and your very first goal live on your dashboard — ready to start measuring progress right away.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Navigate to [trxphy.com](https://trxphy.com) and click **Sign Up** in the top-right corner. Enter your email address and choose a strong password, then click **Create Account** to continue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Open your inbox and look for an email from Trxphy with the subject **Confirm your email address**. Click the verification link inside the email to activate your account. If you don't see it within a couple of minutes, check your spam or junk folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete your profile">
    After verification, Trxphy walks you through a short onboarding flow. Add your display name, upload an avatar, and set your preferences so the dashboard feels like yours from day one. For a full breakdown of every profile option, see the [Account Setup](/account-setup) guide.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first goal">
    From your dashboard, click **+ New Goal** in the upper-right corner. Give your goal a clear title, set a measurable target, and choose a due date. Hit **Save Goal** and your goal appears on the dashboard, ready to track.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Want a deeper walkthrough of the dashboard, milestones, and progress updates? Head over to the [First Steps guide](/guides/first-steps) for a guided tour of each feature.
</Tip>
