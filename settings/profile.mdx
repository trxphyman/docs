Your Trxphy profile is the central place to manage how you appear to teammates and collaborators, as well as how the platform behaves for you. Keeping your profile up to date ensures accurate reporting, correct goal attribution, and a consistent experience across all your devices.

## Personal information

Your personal information controls how you appear throughout Trxphy — in goal feeds, team dashboards, and shared links. To update it, navigate to **Settings → Profile → Personal Info**.

- **Display name** — The full name shown on your goals and activity feed. This can be your real name or any name you prefer.
- **Username** — A unique handle used to identify your account in URLs and @mentions. Choose something short and memorable.
- **Bio** — A brief description of yourself or your role. This is visible to teammates on shared workspaces.
- **Avatar** — Upload a profile photo by clicking the avatar circle and selecting an image from your device. Trxphy accepts JPG, PNG, and WebP files up to 5 MB. Your image is automatically cropped to a square.

<Note>
  Changing your username updates the URL of any goal links you have previously shared. Anyone using an old link will no longer reach your goal — make sure to redistribute updated links after a username change.
</Note>

## Contact information

Your email address is used for login, notifications, and account recovery. To update it, go to **Settings → Profile → Contact Info** and enter your new email address in the **Email** field.

After you save the change, Trxphy sends a verification message to your new address. Your account continues to use your old email until you click the confirmation link in that message. If you do not receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or request a new verification link from the same settings page.

## Preferences

Trxphy's preference settings let you tailor the platform to your location and working style. You can find all of these options under **Settings → Profile → Preferences**.

- **Timezone** — Sets the timezone used for goal deadlines, progress reports, and scheduled notifications. Choosing the correct timezone ensures that daily and weekly summaries arrive at the right local time.
- **Language** — Changes the display language of the Trxphy interface. New languages are added regularly; if your language is not yet available, the interface defaults to English.
- **Date format** — Choose between MM/DD/YYYY, DD/MM/YYYY, and YYYY-MM-DD to match your regional convention. This format is applied consistently across reports, goal cards, and exports.

<Tip>
  To keep your account secure, regularly review your password and enable two-factor authentication. Visit [Security Settings](/settings/security) for step-by-step instructions.
</Tip>
