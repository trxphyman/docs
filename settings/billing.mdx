Trxphy offers three subscription tiers designed to grow with you — whether you are tracking personal goals, running a solo project, or coordinating a whole team. This page walks you through comparing plans, upgrading your subscription, managing payment methods, and accessing your billing history.

## Plans

Choose the plan that fits your goals and team size. All paid plans are billed monthly and can be cancelled at any time.

| Plan | Price | Goals | Team Members | Integrations | Analytics |
|------|-------|-------|--------------|--------------|-----------|
| Free | $0 / mo | 3 goals | 1 user | None | Basic |
| Pro | $9 / mo | Unlimited | 1 user | All integrations | Advanced |
| Team | $25 / mo | Unlimited | Up to 10 users | All integrations | Advanced + team analytics |

## Upgrade your plan

Upgrading takes less than two minutes. Follow these steps to move to a higher tier at any time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings → Billing** from the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Upgrade">
    Select **Upgrade Plan** to open the plan selection screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a plan">
    Review the plan details and click **Choose Plan** under the tier you want — Pro or Team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter payment details">
    Add your credit or debit card information in the secure payment form. Trxphy never stores raw card numbers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your upgrade">
    Click **Confirm Upgrade**. Your new plan activates immediately and you are charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing cycle.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Payment methods

You can store multiple cards on your account and designate one as the default for recurring charges. To add or remove a card, go to **Settings → Billing → Payment Methods**.

- **Add a card** — Click **Add Payment Method**, enter your card details, and click **Save**.
- **Set as default** — Click the three-dot menu next to any saved card and select **Set as Default**.
- **Remove a card** — Click the three-dot menu and select **Remove**. You cannot remove your only card while on a paid plan.

<Warning>
  If you are on a free trial, make sure to add a valid payment method before your trial expires. When a trial ends without a saved card, your account automatically reverts to the Free plan and any data above the Free tier limits becomes read-only.
</Warning>

## Invoices and receipts

Trxphy generates an invoice automatically at the start of each billing cycle. To download past invoices, go to **Settings → Billing → Invoices**. Each invoice is available as a PDF and includes an itemised breakdown of charges, your plan name, and your billing address. You can download any invoice by clicking the **Download PDF** icon next to it in the list.

If you need invoices sent to a specific email address (for example, your company's finance team), add a **Billing email** in the Billing Settings page and all future invoices will be forwarded there automatically.

## Cancel subscription

You can cancel your subscription at any time from the Billing settings page. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you will not be charged again after that date.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings → Billing** from the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Cancel Subscription">
    Scroll to the bottom of the page and click **Cancel Subscription**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a reason">
    Optionally select a cancellation reason from the dropdown. Your feedback helps us improve Trxphy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm cancellation">
    Click **Confirm Cancellation**. You will receive an email confirming the change.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  After your paid plan ends, your account moves to the Free tier. Trxphy retains all your data — including goals, notes, and history — for **30 days**. Within that window you can resubscribe and pick up exactly where you left off. After 30 days, data that exceeds Free plan limits is permanently deleted.
</Note>
