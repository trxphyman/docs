Staying consistent with goal tracking is easier when the right reminders reach you at the right time. Trxphy's notification system lets you choose exactly which events trigger an alert, how you receive it, and how often — so your notifications stay useful rather than becoming noise.

## Notification types

Trxphy supports four categories of notifications. Expand each one to understand what it does and when you'd want to enable it.

<Accordion title="Daily digest">
  The daily digest is a summary email sent once per day that lists all of your active goals, their current progress values, and any milestones due within the next seven days. It's designed to give you a bird's-eye view of your entire goal portfolio in under a minute. You can configure the delivery time to match when you typically start your day.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Milestone reminders">
  Milestone reminders alert you before a milestone's due date so you have time to prepare. You choose how far in advance the reminder fires — options include 1 day, 3 days, 1 week, and 2 weeks before the due date. You can set a different lead time for each milestone, or apply a default across all goals from the Notifications settings page.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Check-in reminders">
  Check-in reminders are recurring nudges that prompt you to log your latest progress. Set the frequency — daily, every other day, weekly, or on specific days of the week — and Trxphy will send a reminder at your chosen time if you haven't already logged a check-in in that window. Keeping check-ins regular produces the most accurate progress charts.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Goal completion">
  The goal completion notification fires when you mark a goal as complete. It's a confirmation that your effort has been recorded and a moment to reflect before moving on. You can also configure Trxphy to send a completion summary that includes your total check-ins, milestone hit rate, and the time elapsed from start to finish.
</Accordion>

## Configure email notifications

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Notifications settings">
    Navigate to **Settings → Notifications** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Email tab">
    Click the **Email** tab at the top of the Notifications page to see all email-specific options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle each notification type">
    Use the toggle next to each notification type — **Daily digest**, **Milestone reminders**, **Check-in reminders**, and **Goal completion** — to enable or disable it individually.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set frequency and timing">
    For each enabled notification, expand its settings panel to choose the delivery frequency and preferred send time. For check-in reminders, also select which days of the week the reminder should fire.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your preferences">
    Click **Save** at the bottom of the Email tab. Your new settings take effect immediately and apply to all current and future goals.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Configure push notifications

Push notifications appear in your browser and, if you've installed the Trxphy mobile app, on your device's lock screen and notification tray.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Notifications settings">
    Navigate to **Settings → Notifications** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Push tab">
    Click the **Push** tab at the top of the Notifications page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable browser notifications">
    Click **Enable browser notifications** and accept the permission prompt your browser displays. If you previously denied this permission, you'll need to reset it in your browser's site settings before Trxphy can send push notifications.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure per-goal push settings">
    Scroll down to the **Per-goal overrides** section. Here you can open any individual goal and choose which notification types are active for that goal specifically — useful for muting low-priority goals without touching your global defaults.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your preferences">
    Click **Save** to apply your push notification configuration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If you use Slack at work, consider routing milestone reminders and check-in nudges there instead of — or in addition to — push notifications. See [Integrations](/guides/integrations) for instructions on connecting your Slack workspace.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  Disabling all notification types means Trxphy will send you no reminders whatsoever. This can cause you to miss upcoming milestone deadlines and fall behind on check-ins without realizing it. If you want to reduce noise, consider lowering frequency rather than turning everything off entirely.
</Warning>
