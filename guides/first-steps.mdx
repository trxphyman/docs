Trxphy is built around a simple loop: set a goal, break it into milestones, check in regularly, and watch your progress unfold. This walkthrough takes you through that entire loop from scratch so you can hit the ground running with your very first goal.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the dashboard">
    Navigate to [app.trxphy.com](https://app.trxphy.com) and sign in to your account. You'll land on the main dashboard, which shows an overview of all your active goals. If this is your first time, the dashboard will be empty and ready for you to get started.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a goal">
    Click the **+ New Goal** button in the top-right corner of the dashboard. A creation panel slides open — fill in the following fields:

    - **Title** — give your goal a clear, actionable name (e.g., "Run a 5K by July")
    - **Description** — add context about why this goal matters to you
    - **Target date** — set the deadline by which you want to achieve it

    Click **Create Goal** to save. Your new goal appears on the dashboard immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add milestones">
    Open your new goal by clicking its title on the dashboard. Inside the goal view, click **Add Milestone** to define the checkpoints that lead to your final target. For each milestone, enter:

    - A short **milestone title**
    - A **due date** for that checkpoint
    - An optional **target value** if you're tracking a measurable metric

    Repeat this for as many milestones as you need. Milestones appear in chronological order in the goal timeline.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log a check-in">
    With your milestones in place, click **Log Check-in** near the top of the goal view. In the check-in form:

    - Enter your **current value** — this is the number or status that represents where you are right now
    - Add a **note** describing what you've done since your last check-in or any blockers you're facing

    Click **Save Check-in**. Trxphy records the entry and updates your progress charts instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View your progress">
    Click the **Analytics** tab at the top of the goal view to open your progress chart. The chart plots every check-in value over time, overlays your milestone markers, and projects a trend line toward your target date. Use this view to spot momentum dips early and adjust your effort accordingly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use **tags** to keep your goals organized across themes like `health`, `career`, or `learning`. Add tags when creating a goal or edit them any time from the goal detail view. You can then filter your dashboard by tag to focus on one area at a time.
</Tip>
