Trxphy organizes your work into goals, breaks those goals into milestones, and surfaces your progress through a live dashboard. This page walks you through each piece of that structure so you know exactly what to expect as you get started.

## The goal lifecycle

Every goal in Trxphy moves through three states as you work toward it. Understanding these states helps you keep your workspace organized and your focus clear.

- **Draft** — A goal you have created but not yet committed to. Use this state to capture ideas, flesh out details, and set a realistic target date before you go public with your intentions.
- **Active** — A goal you are actively working on. Active goals appear on your dashboard, trigger reminders, and accumulate check-in history so you can see your progress over time.
- **Complete** — A goal you have marked as finished. Completed goals move to your archive but remain fully searchable and visible in your analytics, so your history is never lost.

<Note>
  You can move a goal back from **Active** to **Draft** at any time if your priorities shift. Changing a goal's state does not delete any check-ins or milestone data already recorded against it.
</Note>

## Dashboard

The main dashboard is your command center. Every time you log in, it gives you an at-a-glance view of everything that matters:

- **Goal summary cards** — Each active goal gets its own card showing its title, current progress percentage, and the number of milestones remaining. Cards change color as you approach a target date, so deadlines are never a surprise.
- **Progress charts** — A rolling chart displays your check-in history across all active goals, letting you spot momentum trends and identify stretches where activity has slowed.
- **Upcoming milestones** — A chronological list of the next milestones due across all your active goals, so you always know what to focus on this week.

You can pin specific goals to the top of your dashboard and reorder cards by dragging them, keeping the most important work front and center.

## Workspaces

Trxphy separates your work into personal and team workspaces, and you can belong to as many of each as you need.

Your **personal workspace** is private by default — only you can see the goals, milestones, and check-ins you create there. It is the right place for individual goals, private projects, and anything you are not ready to share.

**Team workspaces** are shared environments where every member can view, comment on, and contribute to the same set of goals. Admins control who can join the workspace and what permissions each member holds. Switching between workspaces takes one click from the workspace switcher in the top navigation bar.
