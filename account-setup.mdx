Before you dive into tracking goals, take a few minutes to set up your Trxphy account. A complete profile helps Trxphy personalize your experience, and the right notification and security settings ensure you stay informed and protected as you work toward your goals.

## Profile information

Your profile is how Trxphy identifies you across the platform. Keep the following fields up to date so your dashboard and any shared reports always reflect accurate information.

- **Full name** — your display name shown across the dashboard and any goal collaborations.
- **Username** — a unique handle used to identify your account; you can change it once every 30 days.
- **Avatar** — upload a photo or choose a generated icon to personalize your account.
- **Timezone** — select the timezone that matches your location.

<Note>
  Your timezone setting directly affects how Trxphy calculates deadlines, generates progress reports, and schedules email digests. Set it correctly before you create your first goal to avoid off-by-one day discrepancies in your reporting.
</Note>

To update any of these fields, go to **Settings → Profile** or visit the [Profile settings](/settings/profile) page directly.

## Notification preferences

Trxphy keeps you in the loop through email digests and push notifications. Configure each channel to match how you like to work.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email">
    Email notifications are sent to the address associated with your account. You can customize the following options:

    - **Daily digest** — a summary of goal progress, upcoming deadlines, and suggested actions delivered each morning.
    - **Weekly report** — a broader overview of the week's achievements and areas that need attention, sent every Monday.
    - **Goal milestones** — instant alerts whenever you or a collaborator marks a milestone complete.
    - **Account alerts** — critical messages about billing, security events, and policy updates (cannot be disabled).

    To change your email preferences, go to **Settings → Notifications** and toggle each option on or off under the **Email** section.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Push">
    Push notifications appear on your device in real time and are ideal for staying on top of urgent updates without opening the app.

    - **Goal reminders** — nudges sent at a time you choose to keep you on track with daily targets.
    - **Milestone alerts** — instant notifications when a milestone is completed.
    - **Mentions** — alerts when a collaborator mentions you in a goal comment.
    - **App updates** — occasional announcements about new features and improvements.

    Enable push notifications by going to **Settings → Notifications**, selecting the **Push** tab, and toggling on the events you care about. You may also be prompted to grant notification permissions in your browser or mobile device settings.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Security

Keeping your Trxphy account secure protects both your personal data and the integrity of your goals.

<Warning>
  Never share your password or two-factor authentication codes with anyone — including Trxphy support staff. Anyone with access to your account can modify or delete your goals and personal information. If you suspect unauthorized access, change your password immediately and review your active sessions in [Security settings](/settings/security).
</Warning>

- **Password** — use a unique password of at least 12 characters that you don't reuse on other sites. To update your password, go to **Settings → Security → Change Password**.
- **Two-factor authentication (2FA)** — add a second layer of protection by linking an authenticator app such as Google Authenticator or Authy. Once enabled, you'll be prompted for a one-time code every time you sign in from a new device.

For advanced options — including active session management, trusted devices, and login history — visit the [Security settings](/settings/security) page.
